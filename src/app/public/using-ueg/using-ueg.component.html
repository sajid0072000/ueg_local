1-on-1 online learning

Select Educators for 1-1 online

  • For 1-1 lessons, you may want to begin by browsing our Educator profiles first.
  • Look at one or more of our five departments to find the right option for you: Academic Development, Skill Acquisition, Professional Learning and Wellbeing.
  • Take time to figure out which Educators fit your requirements before you start learning.
  • Each Educator is available for standard 1-1 lessons, or they can design an entirely bespoke course, just for you.
  • Pin the Educators that will best enhance your development or study goals.
  • If you find one or more that suit your requirements, please just click Contact Educator and book in your trial lesson.

Lesson plans exactly as you want them

  • Our Educators can advise on the best course of action for 1-1 lessons. However, there is no reason that students and parents cant draw up a schedule that they wish the Educator to follow.
  • The advantage of 1-1 lessons is that they are entirely catered to you. Below there is some further detail on the 1-1 course experience.
Select Courses for 1-1 online

  • Browse Courses onsite in our four departments.
  • Select Enrol on Course as many times as you see Courses that fit your study plan.
  • Courses can also be adjusted in 1-1 learning if you want to make changes.
  • Book in as many trial lessons as appropriate for your requirements.
Course Details

  • Each one of our courses comes with a specific set of features you can always expect.
  • All courses include homework and feedback, whether this be in the form of presentations delivered in class, tests or marked written work.
  • We always include an introductory video from the Educator about the course. This gives our students the opportunity to have a taste of what lessons are like before signing up for a trial.
  • Although homework and feedback are recommended, as you are taking courses by yourself, any parts of the course can be adjusted to fit your precise goals and requirements.

Group Online Learning

Select Educators for Group Online

Lesson Plans for Everyone

  • If you have created a group, UEG will get clear on what the overall objective is, whether that be hitting a certain target, or just learning for pleasure.
  • The nature of group learning means that there is less flexibility than with a 1-1 lesson. However, the groups Educator will always strive to accommodate wishes as far as possible.
  • Please discuss with your group if you would like to make adjustments to the lesson plan and together, well come up with a solution.
Join a Group Online

  • Alternatively, its possible to join a group online class that is already running, providing that the class is happy to accommodate a new group member.

in-person education

Educators directly to your home

  • Union Education Group is based in London and we can send Educators to your home on request.
  • Educators are available both nationally and internationally for residential stays or one-day visits.
  • In-person Education can take place both in a 1-1 setting and in small groups.
  • Courses can also be taken in person at your home or at another location.

Bespoke Lesson Plans in-Person

  • Browse our catalogue of Educators and press Contact Educator to book lessons to take place in person.
  • Lesson plans can be discussed in advance and a structure put in place for the long or short term.
  • In person lessons can be switched online from time to time if this is more convenient for you.
Group Classes in-Person

  • UEG also runs many group classes both in London and nationally.
  • If you would like a group class in-person, please request an Educator but select the Group option.
  • The majority of these classes take place at students homes. However, UEG also has various alternative locations where the group classes can take place.
