UK STUDENTS


As an Educator at UEG, you’ll have the chance to work with a wide range of exceptional students across the UK. Our principal student base is in London and whether you’re looking to cover school education, university support, adult learning or corporate training, we have keen students who are looking to be connected with first-class Educators. We also work with families outside of London and residential stays are available for Educators who are looking to travel.

When working with UK students, Union Education Group also makes ample use of its resources division. When starting new lessons or courses, our students will often purchase the relevant material from UEG so that they’re best supported in their learning and Educators have everything they need to make their lessons as effective as possible. When delivering your lessons, you will be assisted with anything you need in order to produce the best possible outcomes.

ONLINE COURSES


Union Education Group has established itself via partnerships in countries such as China, India, Vietnam and the United States. Thousands of students across the world use our course platform every month due to the brilliant material provided by our Educators. Our course content is divided into four departments, each offering its own unique perspective on education and providing a holistic take on learning. Whatever your passion, we can find a home for it and connect you with groups of students who are hungry to learn.

The advantage of our international student base is that Educators are able to teach at a time that suits them and aren’t limited solely to working in the after school or work period. Every time zone from early morning till night-time is covered at UEG. Some Educators like to find the time zone that best suits them, while others prefer to carry out a full day of 6-8 hours online teaching. There is no limit to the amount of online teaching Educators can carry out if they want to make it their core focus.

INTERNATIONAL TRAVEL


There are two key types of international travel for teaching purposes at Union Education Group. The first is staying with a family in another country. Ordinarily, this is for a shorter period of time and focused on a specific objective like passing an exam or mastering a language quickly. However, there are instances when this requires a longer commitment of six to twelve months. This is usually when a student has taken time off school or is between jobs and needs more contact hours than would normally be required. Accommodation and travel are covered by the family receiving tuition.

A firmer and longer commitment is required when Educators travel abroad to work in one of our partnership schools or universities. Ordinarily, this would be a fixed contract for at least one year as all accommodation and travel is covered by UEG. There is also scope to live with families rather than in hotels or rented accommodation if Educators are interested in cultural immersion. Mostly, work in international schools and universities is focused on English language training but there are other subjects available to teach primarily in the humanities and sciences.
